\subsubsection*{Problem Statement Question 1}
A biomechanics researcher analyzes a 3D musculoskeletal region where the
\textbf{muscle force density} is given by:
\[ g(x,y,z) = 652.75x^{4}y^{8}z^{7} \]
over the region bounded by: $0 \le x \le 5$, $0 \le y \le 6$, $0 \le z \le 3$.
Compute the total muscle force distributed in the region.

\subsubsection*{Solution}
The total muscle force $F$ is defined as the triple integral of the density function $g(x, y, z)$ over the rectangular region $V$:
\[ F = \iiint_V g(x, y, z) \, dV \]

Given the bounds of the region, the limits of integration are:
\[ 0 \le x \le 5, \quad 0 \le y \le 6, \quad 0 \le z \le 3 \]

Applying the iterated integration process step-by-step:
\[ F = \int_{0}^{3} \int_{0}^{6} \int_{0}^{5} 652.75x^{4}y^{8}z^{7} \, dx \, dy \, dz \]

\vspace{1cm}
\noindent\textbf{Step 1: Integrate with respect to $x$.}

\[ \int_{0}^{5} 652.75x^{4}y^{8}z^{7} \, dx = 652.75y^{8}z^{7} \left[ \frac{x^5}{5} \right]_{0}^{5} \]
\[ = 652.75y^{8}z^{7} \left( \frac{3125}{5} \right) = 652.75y^{8}z^{7} (625) = 407968.75y^{8}z^{7} \]
\[ \Rightarrow F = \int_{0}^{3} \int_{0}^{6} 407968.75y^{8}z^{7} \, dy \, dz \]

\vspace{1cm}
\noindent\textbf{Step 2: Integrate with respect to $y$.}

Next, we integrate the resulting expression with respect to $y$:
\[ \int_{0}^{6} 407968.75y^{8}z^{7} \, dy = 407968.75z^{7} \left[ \frac{y^9}{9} \right]_{0}^{6} \]
Substituting the bounds gives:
\[ = 407968.75z^{7} \left( \frac{10077696}{9} \right) \]
\[ = 407968.75z^{7} (1119744) = 456820500000z^{7} \]
The integral expression simplifies to:
\[ F = \int_{0}^{3} 456820500000z^{7} \, dz \]

\vspace{1cm}
\noindent\textbf{Step 3: Integrate with respect to $z$.}

Finally, we evaluate the outermost integral with respect to $z$:
\[ \int_{0}^{3} 456820500000z^{7} \, dz = 456820500000 \left[ \frac{z^8}{8} \right]_{0}^{3} \]
\[ = 456820500000 \left( \frac{6561}{8} \right) = 456820500000 (820.125) \]

\vspace{1cm}
\noindent\textbf{Step 4: Simplify the result.}

Performing the final arithmetic calculation:
\[ F \approx \boxed{374,649,962,215,039.5000} \]

Thus, the total muscle force distributed in the region is approximately:
\[ \mathbf{374,649,962,215,039.5000} \]
